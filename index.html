I am a mathematician working in the analysis of partial differential equations, currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Ghent Analysis & PDE Center, Ghent University.

Previously I was a postdoctoral research assistant at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Queen Mary University of London, supported by the EPSRC grant EP/V005529/1, where I worked with Claudia Garetto and Michael Ruzhansky. I received my PhD in Mathematics in 2019 from Al-Farabi Kazakh National University and the Institute of Mathematics and Mathematical Modeling in Almaty, under the supervision of Tynysbek Kalmenov and Michael Ruzhansky. Before turning to analysis, I completed an MSc in Aerospace Engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

Research interests

  • Hyperbolic equations and systems with multiplicities
  • PDEs with singular and rough coefficients
  • Functional inequalities on stratified Lie groups
  • Global existence and nonexistence of solutions to PDEs
